### 组合性质
#### 结合律 

$$
h\circ(g\circ f) = (h\circ g)\circ f = h\circ g\circ f
$$

```haskell
f :: A -> B
g :: B -> C
h :: C -> D
h . (g . f) == (h . g) . f == h . g . f -- (“pseudo Haskell” because equality is not defined for functions.)
```
#### 任意对象A都有一个态射指向自己并且可以作为组合的单位元
$$
\displaylines{
f\circ id_A = f\\
id_B\circ f = f
}
$$
